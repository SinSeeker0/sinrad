BOOT VIDEOS
===========
Drop the videos you want to play at startup (a random one plays while the app loads) into THIS folder.
- Supported: mp4, webm, mkv, mov, ogg, ogv, m4v
- They get bundled with the app and copied into your app-data "boot" folder on first run.
- If this folder is empty, no splash video plays and the app opens normally.
